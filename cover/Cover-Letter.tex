Please find enclosed our manuscript entitled as ``{\em Automated Program Verification and Synthesis of Embedded \& Cyber-physical Systems: Advances and Challenges},'' by Lucas Cordeiro, Eddie Batista de Lima Filho, and Iury Bessa, which we would like to submit for publication in the journal of {\bf ACM Transactions on Cyber-Physical Systems (TCPS)}.

\vi

The present work is a survey paper that discusses challenges, problems, and recent advances to ensure correctness and timeliness regarding ECPSs. Reliability issues, in the development of ECPSs, are then considered, as a prominent verification and synthesis application for achieving a correct-by-construction design.

\vi
